What is:

Our Mission at SwipeSaver is to help you save your meal plan Swipes! If you don't have a meal plan, you can use SwipeSaver to check menus before you pay for entrence! (Saving you money!)


We let you make educated decesions on what food is worth swiping for! Check what other students like you rated each item, and the nutritous value of each food you plan to swipe for!


Currently we only plan on releasing the information for "The Commons" but in the future You can see all the dining halls and see which ones are worth your SWIPE!
