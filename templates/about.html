About SwipeSaver

Project Overview

SwipeSaver is designed with the RPI student community in mind. Our primary goal is to offer a user-friendly platform where students can view daily dining hall menus. By doing so, we aim to help students make informed decisions on whether to use or save their dining hall swipes.

Some of the core features of our website include:

  • Access to daily updated menus for all dining halls.
  • A feature to view nutritional information for each food item.
  • A rating system, allowing students to rate meals on a scale of 1 to 5 stars.
  • A weekly highlight of the highest-rated meals.
  • A search functionality to locate specific meals and determine when they'll be served.

We are continuously working to enhance the SwipeSaver experience, ensuring students have the best tools at their disposal for their dining choices.
